## General Information

Sunrise Medical Clinic is a family medical practice located at 450 Oak Avenue, Springfield, IL 62704. The clinic phone number is (217) 555-0142 and the fax number is (217) 555-0143. For emergencies, patients should call 911 or go to the nearest emergency room. The clinic email is contact@sunrisemedical.com and the website is www.sunrisemedical.com.

We accept most major insurance plans including Blue Cross Blue Shield, Aetna, UnitedHealthcare, Cigna, Medicare, and Medicaid. Patients without insurance can discuss self-pay options with the billing department.

## Appointment Scheduling

### Booking an Appointment
Patients can schedule appointments by:
1. Calling (217) 555-0142 during office hours
2. Using the online patient portal at portal.sunrisemedical.com
3. Walking in (subject to availability, longer wait times expected)

New patient appointments require 30 minutes and should be booked at least 3 days in advance. Established patient follow-ups are 15 minutes. Annual physical exams require a 45-minute slot and should be scheduled at least 2 weeks in advance.

### Cancellation Policy
Patients must cancel or reschedule at least 24 hours before their appointment. A $50 no-show fee applies to missed appointments without prior notice. Three consecutive no-shows may result in discharge from the practice.

### Wait Times
- Scheduled appointments: average wait time is 15 minutes
- Walk-in visits: wait times range from 30 to 90 minutes depending on patient volume
- Saturday walk-ins tend to have the longest wait times

## Patient Portal

The online patient portal (portal.sunrisemedical.com) allows patients to:
- View upcoming and past appointments
- Request prescription refills
- Access lab results (available 2-3 business days after testing)
- Send secure messages to their provider
- Update insurance and personal information
- Pay bills online

New patients can register on the portal by entering their date of birth and the registration code provided at their first visit.

## Prescription Refills

Prescription refill requests can be submitted through the patient portal or by calling the office. Please allow 48 hours for refill processing. Controlled substances require an in-person visit and cannot be refilled via phone or portal. The clinic uses CVS Pharmacy at 200 Main Street and Walgreens at 580 Lincoln Avenue as preferred pharmacies.

## Insurance and Billing

The billing department can be reached at (217) 555-0144. Co-pays are collected at the time of the visit. Patients are responsible for any balance after insurance processing. Payment plans are available for balances over $200. We accept cash, checks, and all major credit cards.

## Referrals

For specialized care beyond our scope, we maintain referral relationships with:
- Springfield General Hospital (emergency and inpatient care)
- Springfield Imaging Center (X-rays, MRI, CT scans)
- Heartland Cardiology Associates
- Prairie Orthopedic Specialists
- Central Illinois Dermatology

Referrals typically take 3-5 business days to process. Urgent referrals can be expedited within 24 hours.
